Ready. Connect to QuickBooks to begin.

1. Connection

Not connected

Connect FinAccruals to your accounting platform. For MVP, QuickBooks Online is required.

Once connected, you’ll be able to pull accounts and vendors directly into this workbook.

2. Master Data

Accounts & Vendors

Pull the latest chart of accounts and vendor list into dedicated sheets in this workbook.

Data will be written to Accounts and Vendors sheets. Do not rename these sheets.

3. Journal Entry Template

Excel-driven

Create or refresh the journal entry template sheet with named ranges and dropdowns.

  • Account and vendor columns will use dropdowns from master data.
  • Debits and credits will be validated for balance.

4. Validate & Submit

Review required

Validate the current journal entry and submit it to QuickBooks Online.

5. Submission History

Read-only

View recent submissions from this workbook for quick audit and troubleshooting.
